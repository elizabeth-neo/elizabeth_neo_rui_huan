My Values

Some of the core values I hold close to me is open-mindedness

and personal growth.

I believe that keeping an open mind and listening to other's

point of view is important. This makes the other party feel

feel listened and understood which can turn out to build a

layer of trust. In addition, having a open mind can bring

interesting topics and gain many insights.

Personal growth is a value that resonates a lot with me

because the feeling of a sense of achievement rushes

whenever I feel that I become a better version of myself.

Every year I try to set a new goal for myself and work

towards the goal. This way, I can move on with my life

with my heads up.
